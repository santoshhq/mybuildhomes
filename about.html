About Us

Building Excellence Since 2010

Our Story

My Build Homes has been a trusted name in construction for over a decade, delivering exceptional quality and craftsmanship in every project we undertake.

Founded with a vision to transform the construction industry, we've built our reputation on integrity, innovation, and unwavering commitment to excellence. From residential homes to commercial buildings, our experienced team brings dreams to life with precision and care.

We believe that every project is unique and deserves personalized attention. Our approach combines traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver results that exceed expectations.

Meet Our Founder

Adithya

Founder & CEO

With over 15 years of experience in the construction industry, Adithya founded My Build Homes with a passion for creating spaces that inspire and endure. His hands-on approach and commitment to quality have been the cornerstone of our success.

Adithya's vision extends beyond just building structures; he believes in building relationships and communities. Under his leadership, My Build Homes has completed hundreds of successful projects, earning the trust and loyalty of clients across the region.

"Our mission is simple: to build homes that families cherish for generations. Every nail, every beam, every detail matters because your dreams matter."
